Shi Hu


"Only when you are drunk
do you know that wine is intense.
Only when you have loved someone else
do you know how heavy the feelings are.
You can't be my poem.
I can't be your dream either."

You will only know how deep the love is
after separating from the person you love.


“醉过才知酒浓，
爱过才知情重，
你不能做我的诗，
正如我不能做你的梦。”

胡适Shi Hu
1891.12.17-1962.2.24
