Guangzhong Yu & Wocun Fan


"Don't ask me if I have you in my heart,
and you are also in the afterglow of my eyes.
You are in everything I have."
In Chinese, the afterglow of the eyes
and the author's name is the same three Chinese characters.
The author makes a double entendre.


“别问我心里有没有你，
我余光中全是你。”
