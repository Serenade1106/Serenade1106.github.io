Xun Lu & Guangping Xu


“Whenever I want to send you a letter,
I must send it to the post office first.
I dislike putting my notes in the green post box down the street.
Because I would suspect that the post office would become very slow in delivering the letters. Although the letter delivery is slow, I miss you very urgently.”


“我寄你的信，
总要送往邮局，
不喜欢放在街边的绿色邮简中，
我总疑心那里会慢一点。
信很慢，
可我的心很快。”

鲁迅Xun Lu
1881.9.25-1936.10.19
