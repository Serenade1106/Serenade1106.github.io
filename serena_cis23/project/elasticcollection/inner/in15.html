Yiduo Wen & Xiaozhen Gao


“When I got up this morning, I cleared the weeds around our house.
I thought you would be happy to see it when you came back.
The lotus flower must wait for you to return before it blooms.
Everything I do is for you. Everything in the world exists because of you.”


“今天早晨起来拔了半天草，
心里想到等你回来看着高兴，
荷花也放了苞，
大概也要等你回来开，
一切都是为你。”

闻一多Yiduo Wen
1899.11.24-1946.7.15
