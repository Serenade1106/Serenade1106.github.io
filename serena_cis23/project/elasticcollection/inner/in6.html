Mao San & Xi He


"If I keep thinking of you non-stop,
you will float down so much sand and
water drops to form the Sahara desert and the Pacific Ocean
because the amount of sand in the
Sahara Desert and the amount of water drops
in the Pacific Ocean is uncountable."


“每想你一次，
天上飘落一粒沙，
从此形成了撒哈拉。
每想你一次，
天上就掉下一滴水，
子是形成了太平洋。”

三毛Mao San
1943.3.26-1991.1.4
