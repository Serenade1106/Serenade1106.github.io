Ziqing Zhu & Zhuyin Chen


"I love your eyes, and when I see your eyes,
I become awake.
But I like your cheeks even more when you are shy,
like the evening sun at dusk.
Thank you for the strength you bring to me."


“一见你的眼晴，
我便清醒起来，
我更喜欢看你那晕红的双腮，
黄昏时的霞彩似的，
谢谢你给我力量。”

朱自清Ziqing Zhu
1898.11.22-1948.8.12
