Zi Hai


"The one I love, tonight I don't care about humans.
I only know that I miss you."
The person the author loves is older than he is,
so he chooses to call her his sister.
The fact that he doesn't care about humans means
that he doesn't care about everything in the world.
He only cares and misses the person he loves.


“姐姐，
今晚我不关心人类，
我只想你。”

海子Zi Hai
1964.3.24-1989.3.26
