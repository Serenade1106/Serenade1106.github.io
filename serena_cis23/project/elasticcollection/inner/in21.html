Xiang Zhu & Nijun Liu


"I wish these years had gone faster
because I wanted to get back to
the person I loved as soon as possible.
I can feel the warmth of her body
when I sleep near her at night.
I would be satisfied with whispering
sweet words and putting my arms around her waist. "


“唉，
要是我快点过了这几年，
到霓妹妹身边，
晚上挨着她睡下，
沾她一点热气，
低低說些情话，
拿一只臂膀围起她那腰身，
我就心满意足了。”

朱湘 Xiang Zhu
1904-1933.12.5
