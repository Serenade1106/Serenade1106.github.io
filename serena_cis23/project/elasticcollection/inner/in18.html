Xin Bing & Wenzao Wu


"On my way to you.The sun is warm.
The clouds are lovely.And the evening breeze is sweet."
The author wants to express that before two people
who love each other are about to meet,
they will feel that everything around them is beautiful
and sweet because it is exciting and happy to see the person you love."


“见你的路上，
阳光温热，
云朵可爱，
想必晚风吹来，
也是甜的。”

冰心 Xin Bing
1900.10.5-1999.2.28
