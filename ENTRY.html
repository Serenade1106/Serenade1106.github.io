Social_cognition.is the process by which individuals make speculations and judgments about the mental states, behavioral motivations, and intentions of others.


PREDICT DECISIONS AND CHOICES

In some cases, we can predict people's behavior by their attitudes. For example, when we minimize factors such as unexpected events in our lives, we find that certain behaviors that individuals typically exhibit are more likely to be predicted and occur. When a person's attitudes and beliefs are strong enough, that person's behavior is more likely to be predicted. Or when the person's attitude is closely related to the predicted behavior, then that behavior is also more likely to be predicted.
